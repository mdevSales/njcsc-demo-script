🚀 Demo Summary

One storyline, one platform, three personas, tuned for Joe at the New Jersey Civil Service Commission. Follow Paul Marshall — a Rutgers CS senior the CSC Office of Outreach & Engagement met at a Bergen County career expo. He drives Vignette 1 (candidate). Anna Carter at the NJ Office of Information Technology picks him up in Vignette 2 (agency liaison). Jane Jones in NJCSC Central operates the platform end-to-end in Vignette 3 — bookended with analytics so leadership sees the outcome before and after the walkthrough.

~30 minutes total. Three vignettes. Same records, three surfaces, one platform.

Framing for Joe: Joe cares less about "can Salesforce do this" and more about "is this a platform we can grow on." Every vignette below has a Platform Beat (the tech) and, on key steps, a Joe Beat (the enterprise-scale implication). Deliver both. The demo is the same demo — the narration is what makes it Joe's demo.
